Caged Cowboys is a local multi-player cowboy space revolver brawler! Play 2 - 4 players using XBox 360 controllers or PlayStation 4 controllers.
It was built during Dreamhackathon 2014 in just 24 hours (plus some post-event fixing before putting it online).
