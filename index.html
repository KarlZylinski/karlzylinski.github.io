Bitsquid is a game engine for PC, consoles and handhelds that offers, by being highly data-driven, the flexibility needed to create a wide variety of games. Other areas of focus are short iteration times and collaboration.

Currently, I mainly focus on creating and maintaining tools and any runtime systems required by those. But I've also worked on things such as improving animation systems, multi-platform texture compression and making the engine support 64-bit.
